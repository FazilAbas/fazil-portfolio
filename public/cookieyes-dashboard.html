What Drives Retention

LinkedIn users stay longest, Google Ads users churn fastest

Active Subscription Rate by Source

LinkedIn 43.1% vs Google Ads 36.7% retention

Volume vs Quality tradeoff: Google Ads drives 33.5% of signups but has the worst retention (36.7%). LinkedIn drives 17% of signups with the best retention (43.1%). Consider shifting budget toward higher-quality channels.

Strategic Recommendations

Data-backed priorities for the next 90 days

1. Fix Activation Flow

Impact: High | Effort: Medium

60.5% of signups never subscribe. Redesign onboarding to guide users to their first value moment within 5 minutes. Expected outcome: 10-15% improvement in subscription rate.

2. Pause Campaign C

Impact: High | Effort: Low

Lowest ROAS (0.09x) means poor returns on every dollar. Pause immediately and reallocate to Campaign B (best ROAS at 0.45x). Expected outcome: $100K+ annual savings.

3. Fix Invoice Failures

Impact: Medium | Effort: Low

24.7% invoice failure rate is leaking revenue. Implement dunning automation (retry logic, payment prompts). Expected outcome: recover $27K+ annually in failed payments.
